Additional Information:
  • Acceptable items include all gear with names that are not yellow in their help window.
  • Items must be of Aetherial, Green, or Blue rarity.
  • The item must be sellable to vendors, which is indicated by a "Sells for" value in its tooltip.
  • For Company Credits, HQ items yield double the amount when compared to NQ items.

About This Tool

This FC turn in calculator helps determine how many items are needed to reach FC Rank 6.

The calculations are based on in-game formulas for Company Credits earned from item turn-ins.

This web tool version was created to make these calculations more accessible than using in-game scripts.
